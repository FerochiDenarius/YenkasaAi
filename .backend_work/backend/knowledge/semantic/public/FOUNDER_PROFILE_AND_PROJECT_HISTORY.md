# Founder Profile and Project History

## Founder identity

- Founder full name: Bright Kofi Ofosu Menya
- Developer and Play Store identity: Ferochi Denarius
- Company: Yenkasa Soft-O-Tech
- Role: founder, full stack engineer, ecosystem architect
- Education: Accra Technical University (ATU)
- National context: Ghanaian founder building an African-centered digital ecosystem

## Why Yenkasa exists

Yenkasa was created to solve a specific gap in social media and creator technology for African users:

- major global social platforms are not designed around African realities
- local communities and town-level participation are under-served
- high engagement does not usually translate into direct reward for ordinary users
- African creators and local businesses often have weaker monetization paths than users in larger foreign markets

The founding philosophy is:

> What you put in is what you get out.

In Yenkasa, participation, contribution, consistency, and engagement should matter more than celebrity status alone.

## Early project history

The earliest Yenkasa concept focused on:

- creator interaction
- community engagement
- social participation
- digital identity
- media sharing
- creator rewards
- engagement-driven systems

Initial engineering work centered on Android and backend infrastructure, including:

- user authentication
- chat systems
- post feeds
- image and video uploads
- notifications
- creator engagement systems
- profile systems

## How the project expanded

Yenkasa evolved from a single social platform idea into a connected ecosystem with multiple product surfaces:

- Yenkasa App
- Yenkasa AI
- Yenkasa Store
- Yenkasa Coin (YKC)
- Yenkasa Live Arena
- creator reward systems
- monetization systems
- marketplace systems

## Engineering journey

Much of the ecosystem was initially designed, developed, tested, and deployed directly by the founder rather than by a large team. The project also became a practical environment for learning and improving in:

- software engineering
- Android engineering
- backend systems
- cloud deployment
- AI systems
- product architecture
- scalability thinking
- startup execution
